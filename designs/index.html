Saltwater Tavern

Web Design and Development

I designed and developed this website for a new restaurant. I figured most people go to a restaurants website if they've never been to the actual establishment before. I put the scrolling photo gallery front and center so people could get a feel for the restaurant. I also put the hours, address and contact information right on the front page because I wanted the "distance" between the user and the information they're seeking to be as little as possible.
